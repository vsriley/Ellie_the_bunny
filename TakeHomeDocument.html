How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Gather supplies (bread, jelly, peanut butter, knife, plate)
  2. Open bag containing bread and place one slice of bread on plate
  3. Open peanut butter container. Use knife to obtain peanut butter from jar. Spread peanut butter on bread using knife until it is evenly spread.
  4. Open jelly container. Use knife to obtain jelly from jar. Spread jelly on bread using knife until it is evenly spread.
  5. Place second slice of bread on top
